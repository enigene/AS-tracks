<h2>Description</h2>
<p>Homo sapiens Alpha Satellites Map</p>
<p><b>Sequencing/Assembly name:</b> GRCh38</p>
<p><b>Assembly date:</b> 2013-12-17</p>
<p><b>GenBank accession ID:</b> <a href="http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/assembly/GCA_000001405.15/" target="_blank">GCA_000001405.15</a></p>

<h2>Methods</h2>
<p>Alpha Satellite monomers were identified by PERCON similarity search, extracted and distributed into the classes characteristic of the 5 suprachromosomal families by a Bayesian classifier.</p>
